About Me

As you can clearly see from this website, I have a lot of very diverse interests. I love theatre and acting, I enjoy filmmaking and directing, I love books, and writing, and poetry, and I also love coding, and games, and design. Throughout my time in highschool, and even earlier than that, I’ve devoted my summers and my spare time to the pursuit of these interests, and have created a number of different projects of varying merit, that have at last found a common home here on this website. There are some things on this website that I have been doing since before I can remember.

I’ve been writing forever, both in school, and out of school. As a child I would invent stories of my own, sometimes on paper, and sometimes simply in my own head. That continued as I entered high school, and I began to further hone that talent, writing a number of things, some of which are presented here, others of which remain unpublished, but which, nonetheless helped me develop my skill in writing and my distinct voice.

I’ve also been acting forever; I’ve been coding since 7th or 8th grade; and I started my first (albeit very silly) company in 8th grade. Enjoy the things that I have spent my life thus far creating, and help me to refine my talents by sending me feedback at calvinj.degraaf@gmail.com
